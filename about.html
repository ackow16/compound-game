About COMPOUND

A daily word chain puzzle game

What is COMPOUND?

COMPOUND is a free daily word puzzle where you arrange words to form a continuous chain of compound words around a board. Each adjacent pair of words must combine to create a valid compound word.

SUN → LIGHT → HOUSE SUN + LIGHT = sunlight  |  LIGHT + HOUSE = lighthouse

How to Play

  1. Start with 16 words in the center of the board
  2. Drag words to the slots around the edge
  3. Form a chain where each adjacent pair creates a compound word
  4. Complete the loop - the last word must connect back to the first!
  5. You have 3 attempts to solve each puzzle

A New Puzzle Every Day

Like your favorite daily word games, COMPOUND offers a fresh puzzle every day. Challenge yourself to maintain a streak and share your results with friends!

Who Made This?

COMPOUND was created by @playcompound as a fun daily brain exercise for word game enthusiasts. We believe puzzles should be accessible, enjoyable, and just challenging enough to make you think.
