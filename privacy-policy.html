Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 8, 2025

Welcome to COMPOUND ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at playcompound.com.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our game:

  • Game Progress: Your puzzle statistics, streaks, and game state are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to understand how players interact with the game.

Advertising

We may use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google and its partners may collect and use data about your visits to this and other websites to provide relevant advertisements. This may include:

  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Device identifiers
  • IP address and location data
  • Browsing behavior and interests

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit Google's Privacy Policy and How Google Uses Information.

Cookies

We and our advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic and usage
  • Deliver personalized advertisements
  • Measure ad performance

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or through Google's ad personalization controls at adssettings.google.com.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Opting out of personalized advertising

To opt out of personalized ads, you can visit Google Ad Settings or use the DAA's opt-out tool at optout.aboutads.info.

Data Retention

Game data stored in your browser remains until you clear your browser data.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at playcompoundgame@gmail.com.
